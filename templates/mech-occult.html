Mech-Occult

In a top-secret military installation, a catastrophic containment breach has unleashed unknowable horrors from the void. With humanity having abandoned the facility, the last line of defense is an unlikely one: a lone "Cleaner Droid." In Mech-Occult, an isometric arena shooter, players take on this critical role. You must courageously battle relentless waves of grotesque entities across the desolate floors of the military base. The primary objective is a race against time: survive the onslaught, seal the breach, and prevent a global catastrophe before all hope is lost.

This is a solo passion project currently in active development. Creating Mech-Occult has been a rewarding journey of skill development and creative expression. While many mechanics, features, and visual elements are still in the pipeline, a playable demo is now available, offering a glimpse into the core gameplay experience.
