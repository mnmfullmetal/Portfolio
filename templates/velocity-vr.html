Velocity VR

Velocity VR is a survival horror VR game that places the player on an abandoned colony space craft, wheelchair bound the player must escape the ship while evading the enemy AI, an intelligent and hostile extraterrestrial that is determined to wipe out all life forms onboard.

I made this game, alongside a team of 3 programmers and 2 artists, for AIE during the major project module of my Design course. It was a 14 week project and was a brilliant exercise in not only scoping and time management, but also in pushing ourselves as developers as none of us had ever undertaken a VR project before. Overall the project was a success and exceedingly valuable to me, for many reasons, but the one that stands out the most is I got to experience what it was like leading a production to completion with a team that I hold very dear to my heart.
